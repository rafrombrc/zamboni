Do's

  • provide a label.
  • use it when you have a binary setting because it's cognitively less demanding than a checkbox.

Dont's

  • don't rename the on/off label unless you have a good reason.
  • don't use it when you have more than two values. Use radio buttons or checkboxes instead.
